Application Procedure

Before You Apply:

Parents interested in Washington University Nursery School should contact the school to schedule a visit.

Applying to WUNS:

Fill out the Application for Admission online. You may also print out and return the pdf version: Application for Admission WUNS. The Application for Admission form only needs to be filled out once. An application fee of $40 is required when applying to the nursery school. After these are received, your child is placed on an enrollment list.

Acceptance:

You will be notified of acceptance in the March preceding the fall enrollment date. Upon acceptance, a nonrefundable deposit of one month’s tuition is due. This will be applied to tuition for the following May. You must fill the Developmental History Form out after admission into WUNS. This form only needs to be filled out once. Additionally, the Enrollment Form needs to be filled out every year that your child attends WUNS. In addition, two medical forms (below) are only available as PDFs and must also be filled out and returned:

Child Medical Examination Report

Immunization Consent/History
